My Life in Short

From a village childhood to engineering research – a continuous journey of learning and growth.

Childhood & Early Education

I lived 13 years in my village and studied there up to 6th class. After that I spent one year in Odagaon for my 7th class. Later, I moved to Bhubaneswar and continued my schooling at Saraswati Shishu Vidya Mandir, Niladri Vihar, completing 8th, 9th and 10th with 80% marks.

Engineering Journey

I completed my Diploma in Electrical Engineering from KIIT Polytechnic with 92% and am now pursuing my B-Tech in Electrical Engineering at KIIT University, currently in my final year with a CGPA of 8.15. Along the way, I’ve worked on multiple projects, internships and research publications.
