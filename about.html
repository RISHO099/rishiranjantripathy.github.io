About Me

Who I am beyond grades, projects and designations.

I am Rishi Ranjan Tripathy, an electrical engineer in the making, driven by a strong interest in power systems, power electronics and automation. I enjoy understanding how things work from the inside and using that knowledge to design smarter and more reliable solutions.

My career objective is to gain hands-on experience in cutting-edge research and development in the field of electrical engineering, while contributing my knowledge and technical skills towards meaningful innovation and sustainable energy systems.

Childhood Journey

From village roots to a global learning mindset.

I spent the first 13 years of my life in my village, where I completed my education till 6th class. That period shaped my values, discipline and connection with people and nature. Afterwards, I moved to Odagaon for one year to study in 7th class.

Later, I shifted to Bhubaneswar and continued my schooling at Saraswati Shishu Vidya Mandir, Niladri Vihar, where I studied in 8th, 9th and 10th standards and passed my 10th Board with 80% marks. From there, my journey into electrical engineering truly began.
